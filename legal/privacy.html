Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 22, 2025

Moiz Ahmed ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by the Anar mobile application.

1. The Short Version

We believe your financial data belongs to you. Anar stores your portfolios, watchlists, and budget data locally on your device. We do not store your personal financial data on our servers.

2. Information We Collect

A. Usage and Diagnostics Data (Automated)

To improve the stability and performance of the app, we use third-party services (Firebase) to collect anonymous usage data. This does not include your financial details.

  • Crash Logs: If the app crashes, we receive a log allowing us to fix the bug.
  • Performance Data: We monitor app launch times and responsiveness.
  • Device Information: Device type (e.g., iPhone 15), operating system version, and general location (country/region).

B. Feedback (Voluntary)

If you choose to submit feedback through the app, we may collect:

  • Email Address (Optional): If you choose to provide it, we use it solely to reply to your feedback.
  • Message Content: The text of your feedback or bug report.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the limited data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To identify and fix software bugs (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • To analyze app usage trends (via Firebase Analytics) to improve features.
  • To securely fetch stock market data (via Firebase Cloud Functions).
  • To communicate with you regarding your feedback.

4. Third-Party Services

We use services provided by Google (Firebase) to run the app. Their use of information is governed by their respective privacy policies.

  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics: Used for bug tracking, crash reporting, and usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: We use this solely as a secure "proxy" to fetch stock market data from our data provider. When you search for a stock, the request routes through Firebase to protect our API keys. We do not store your search queries, analytics, or financial data on Firebase. Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and iCloud

Your personal financial data (portfolios, transactions, budgets) is stored locally on your device.

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your data will be backed up to Apple's iCloud servers. This storage is governed by Apple's privacy policy and security standards. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

Incidental Collection via Feedback: The app includes an optional feedback form where users may voluntarily provide an email address. We do not verify the age of users submitting feedback. If a child under 13 submits feedback and provides their email address, this information is collected solely for the purpose of responding to the specific inquiry or bug report. We do not use this email for marketing purposes.

If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with information without their consent, please contact us at the email below. We will delete such information from our records within a reasonable time.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal financial data on our servers, we cannot "delete" your account because we do not have one. You can delete all your data simply by uninstalling the application from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email.
